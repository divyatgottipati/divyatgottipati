Digital Consulting Project with Tailgaters

Led the development of a data-driven digital marketing strategy to enhance Tailgaters Bar and Grill's social media presence, improve customer retention, and drive sales. Utilized data analytics tools to uncover insights and inform strategic decisions.

Overview:

This project focused on improving Tailgaters Bar and Grill's digital presence by leveraging data analytics to create a comprehensive social media strategy. The goal was to increase brand visibility, customer engagement, and overall business performance through targeted campaigns and loyalty programs.

Technologies:

Tableau, Google Analytics, Alteryx, Facebook Business Manager, Instagram Insights, Canva, SurveyMonkey

KEY CONTRIBUTIONS:

  • Conducted in-depth analysis of Tailgaters' existing marketing strategies using Tableau and Google Analytics.
  • Developed a tailored social media content calendar based on audience insights.
  • Designed and implemented customer feedback surveys to gather actionable data.
  • Proposed loyalty programs to drive customer retention and repeat business.
  • Managed and analyzed social media campaigns using Facebook Business Manager and Instagram Insights.

Outcome:

  • Significant improvements in social media engagement, increased foot traffic, and measurable growth in sales. Enhanced customer retention through personalized marketing efforts and loyalty initiatives.
